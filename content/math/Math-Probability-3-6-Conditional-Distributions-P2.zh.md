$$
f(x,y)=g_1(x|y)f_2(y)
$$
where $f$ is the joint p.f. or p.d.f.,or p.f./p.d.f. of $X$ and $Y$ ,Similarly,for each $x$ such that $f_1(x)>0$ and each $y$,
$$
f(x,y)=g_2(y|x)f_1(x)
$$
